How have things changed since the UK went into lockdown?
Since the UK started its coronavirus lockdown, we want to hear how you feel a month on
The end of April will mark one month since the UK went into lockdown. Since the government introduced restrictions such as non-essential travel and physical distancing, we want to hear how things have changed for you.
Whether in work or home life, how has your life been affected? How have your habits changed over time? What do you find difficult and what has improved? Perhaps you have let go of the old and have started afresh. How do you feel now compared to when the lockdown started?
You can get in touch by filling in the form below. Your responses are secure as the form is encrypted and only the Guardian has access to your contributions.
One of our journalists will be in contact for publication before we publish, so please do leave contact details.
