Do you have any concerns about taking a Covid-19 vaccine in the UK?
We would like to hear from people who have concerns, or have changed their mind, about taking a Covid-19 vaccine
A coronavirus vaccine which has been in development by AstraZeneca and a team from Oxford University, has been put on hold due to a participant experiencing an adverse reaction during the trial.
We would like to know whether you have concerns about taking a vaccine.
Has the news of the halting of the Oxford trial impacted on your feelings about taking a Covid-19 vaccine? Has your attitude to the government's handling of the pandemic influenced your willingness to take or not take a vaccine?
