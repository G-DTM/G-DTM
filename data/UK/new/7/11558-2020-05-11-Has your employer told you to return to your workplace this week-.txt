Has your employer told you to return to your workplace this week?
If you're preparing to go back to work – away from home – we'd like to hear from you. How will you travel? Do you have any concerns?
The government's plan to ease the lockdown has provoked confusion. Boris Johnson on Sunday said people in England should go to work if they cannot work from home, but although many people never stopped going to their workplace – particularly in construction and delivery warehouses – furloughed workers are now being ordered to return.
