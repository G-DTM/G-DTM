Share your tributes and memories of UK coronavirus victims who died during the summer
We would like you to share your tributes for friends and family who have died during the summer months
Covid-19 has claimed the lives of thousands of people in the UK.
Lockdown restrictions eased during the summer months as the numbers of fatalities, caused by coronavirus decreased, however we would like to pay tribute to people in the UK who died during this period.
If you'd like to share a tribute to someone who died at another time, please do so here.
We would like you to share your memories and tributes for those who have died from Covid-19 in the UK during the months of June, July, August and early September. You can use the encrypted form below.
One of our journalists will be in contact for publication before we publish, so please do leave contact details.
