Coronavirus in the UK: what precautions are you taking?
We'd like to hear how readers are reacting to Boris Johnson's announcement that new rules could be necessary for six months
As the UK heads towards a winter with new but differing restrictions in the four nations, we would like to hear how people are preparing for the months ahead.
Have you amended plans? Have you temporarily changed a part of your routine to avoid certain places? Have you started stockpiling food or medical supplies, or ordered protective face masks?
