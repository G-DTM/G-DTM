Johnson faces public and Tory revolt over Cummings: Politics Weekly podcast
Jonathan Freedland and Rowena Mason discuss developments in the Dominic Cummings scandal, and Robert Colvile examines the pandemic's impact on UK-EU Brexit negotiations
Jonathan Freedland and Rowena Mason look at whether the government can ride out the latest scandal to rock No 10, and ask why the prime minister is risking so much to protect one senior adviser.
Plus, Jill Rutter, of UK in a Changing Europe, and Robert Colvile, of the centre-right Centre for Policy Studies thinktank, examine whether the UK government's perspective on Brexit talks should change on the back of a coronavirus pandemic.
