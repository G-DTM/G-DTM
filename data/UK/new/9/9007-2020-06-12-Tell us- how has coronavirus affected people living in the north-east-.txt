Tell us: how has coronavirus affected people living in the north-east?
We'd like to hear from people in the north-east and Yorkshire about the economic impact of the coronavirus outbreak
We would like to find out about the impact of coronavirus on you and your community.
We'd like to hear from people in north-east England including the county of Yorkshire and we're particularly interested finding out how the pandemic has affected your work and the local economy. What has changed? How do you view your future?
We also want to hear about people and initiatives that are having a positive impact on the community. Who are the people that you think we should speak to? What are the grassroots projects or initiatives that are making a difference to people's lives that you think will succeed longterm? Are there any support groups for people who have been affected economically?
