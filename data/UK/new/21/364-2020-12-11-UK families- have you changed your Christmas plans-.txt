UK families: have you changed your Christmas plans?
We want to know if you have decided to no longer meet other households or vice versa
As Christmas looms, families are making decisions about who they will see after the government announced rules allowing three households to mix together from 23 to 27 December.
We want to know whether you, your family or friends originally decided to meet over Christmas but have now changed your minds. What are the reasons for no longer mixing households?
You can get in touch by filling in the form below. Your responses are secure as the form is encrypted and only the Guardian has access to your contributions. One of our journalists will be in contact before we publish, so please do leave contact details.
