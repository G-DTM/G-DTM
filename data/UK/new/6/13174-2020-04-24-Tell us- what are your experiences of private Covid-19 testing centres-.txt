If you've used a private coronavirus testing centre, we'd like to find out what the service was like. Did you get your results quickly? Were there any issues?
Lost NHS staff test results, or results sent to the wrong person, has led to hospitals seeking to take over the running of a flagship government coronavirus testing centre operated by the accounting firm Deloitte.
Deloitte is understood to be handling logistics across a number of planned regional testing sites, working alongside other private firms such as Serco and Boots.
