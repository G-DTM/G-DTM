UK taxi and delivery drivers: how have you been affected by coronavirus?
If you work as a food or goods delivery courier or a taxi driver in the UK, we'd like to hear about your experiences
Whether you deliver packages for online retailers, drop off supermarket home deliveries, or drive people from A to B, we'd like to hear how your work has been affected by the coronavirus pandemic.
How have things changed since lockdown started? Do you have any concerns? Do you feel supported by the company you work for?
