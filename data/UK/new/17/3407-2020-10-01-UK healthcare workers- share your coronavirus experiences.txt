UK healthcare workers: share your coronavirus experiences
We would like to hear from those who are working in the healthcare sector about their thoughts and experiences
As health minister Nadine Dorries warns that hospital admissions in England are just 10 days away from reaching a "critical" stage because of rising coronavirus cases, we'd like to hear how UK healthcare workers feel about the months ahead.
With winter approaching, do you feel prepared? How do you feel about your work conditions? Are there any particular areas of concern?
You can get in touch by filling in the form below, anonymously if you wish or via WhatsApp by clicking here or adding the contact +44(0)7867825056. Your responses are secure as the form is encrypted and only the Guardian has access to your contributions.
One of our journalists will be in contact for publication before we publish, so please do leave contact details.
