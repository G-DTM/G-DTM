Government errors pile up: Politics Weekly podcast
Heather Stewart and Peter Walker discuss the latest from Westminster. Plus Anita Boateng and Paul Harrison review the prime minister's performance at the Conservative party conference
Boris Johnson pledges to transform Britain into a "new Jerusalem", meanwhile his government is thwarted by an Excel spreadsheet. Heather Stewart and Peter Walker examine the situation.
Plus, Anita Boateng, the Conservative councillor for Redbridge and former special adviser to the Cabinet Office, and Paul Harrison, the former press secretary to Theresa May, review the Tory conference and discuss whether or not the party has confidence in Johnson.
