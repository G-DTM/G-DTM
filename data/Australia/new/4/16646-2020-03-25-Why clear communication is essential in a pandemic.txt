Why clear communication is essential in a pandemic
As the coronavirus continues to spread, Australians have voiced concern that the government is failing to clearly communicate what steps people should take to slow the rate of infection. In this episode of Full Story, Melissa Davey looks at what other countries have been doing and explains how the Morrison government can do better
