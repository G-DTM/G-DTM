How the Ruby Princess cruise ship debacle spread coronavirus across Australia
On a warm autumn day in Sydney Harbour a cruise ship docked at Circular Quay to let its 2,700 passengers off. Soon this ship would become infamous, as passenger after passenger was confirmed to have contracted Covid-19. In this episode of Full Story we look at what happened on the ship and who was ultimately responsible for a breakdown in Australia's biosecurity safeguards
